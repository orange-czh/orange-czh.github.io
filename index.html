Research

I am Zihan Cheng. I received my B.Sc. degree both in Computer Science at Nanjing University in 2024 from the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Nanjing University. Now, I am a student at the Reasoning and Learning Group and a research assistant at the National Institute of Healthcare Data Science at Nanjing University. I am fortunate enough to be guided by Professor Yinghuan Shi. I am also pursuing the Doctor of Medicine，M.D. degree at the Shanghai Jiao Tong University School of Medicine.
At present, my research interest is in artificial intelligence, especially the application of computer vision in clinical medical diagnosis.
