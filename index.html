Research

I am Zihan Cheng. I received my B.Sc. degree in Computer Science and Technology at Nanjing University in 2024 from the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Nanjing University. Now, I am pursing M.D. (Doctor of Medicine, 医学博士) degree at Shanghai Jiao Tong University School of Medicine and a Research Assistant at the National Institute of Healthcare Data Science at Nanjing University. I am fortunate enough to be guided by Professor Yinghuan Shi since April 2022.
At present, my research interest lies in the field of computer vision, especially the model generalization capabilities in clinical medical diagnosis.
