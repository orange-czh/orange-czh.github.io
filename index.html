About

I received my B.Sc. degree in Computer Science and Technology from the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Nanjing University in 2024.

I am currently pursuing the M.D. degree at Shanghai Jiao Tong University School of Medicine and serving as a Research Assistant at the National Institute of Healthcare Data Science, Nanjing University. Since April 2022, I have been fortunate to work under the supervision of Prof. Yinghuan Shi.

Research

My research focuses on trustworthy and generalizable machine learning for clinical medical diagnosis, with a particular interest in computer vision methods for medical image segmentation and analysis.

  • Medical Image Analysis
  • Computer Vision
  • Model Generalization
